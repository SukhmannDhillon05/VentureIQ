# 📈 VentureIQ – Business Intelligence & Market Analysis Platform

VentureIQ is a data-driven business intelligence platform that evaluates startup and business ideas using market research, competitive analysis, and quantitative scoring. The project helps entrepreneurs make informed decisions by transforming raw market data into actionable business insights.

Developed using **Python**, **SQL**, and **Pandas**, VentureIQ demonstrates practical applications of **Data Analysis**, **Business Intelligence**, and **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)**.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

Choosing the right business idea often depends on understanding market demand, competition, and growth potential. VentureIQ simplifies this process by analyzing structured business data and generating an overall feasibility score based on predefined analytical parameters.

The platform enables users to:

- Evaluate new business ideas
- Analyze market demand
- Compare competitor strength
- Measure business viability
- Generate analytical reports for decision-making

## 📊 Workflow

1. User enters a business idea.
2. Market-related information is collected.
3. Data is cleaned and processed.
4. Market demand and competitor data are analyzed.
5. Business viability score is calculated.
6. Analytical insights are generated.
7. A final report helps users make informed business decisions.
